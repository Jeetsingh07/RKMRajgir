About ramkrishna mission ashram ,rajgir

Ramakrishna Math, Rajgir, is a branch of the Ramakrishna Order founded by Swami Vivekananda, inspired by the teachings of Sri Ramakrishna. Located in the spiritually significant town of Rajgir, Bihar, the Math serves as a peaceful center for meditation, worship, and spiritual learning. It promotes harmony and selfless service through spiritual and humanitarian activities. The ashram also offers limited guest accommodation on a donation basis. Visitors are advised to contact in advance. Nearby attractions include Vishwa Shanti Stupa and ancient Buddhist sites. The Math reflects the ideals of Vedanta and works towards uplifting individuals through spiritual and moral education.

The Ramakrishna Mission Ashrama in Rajgir is renowned for its vibrant events and activities that foster spiritual growth and community engagement. Throughout the year, the ashrama hosts a variety of events including spiritual discourses, meditation sessions, cultural programs, and educational workshops. These events are designed to promote the teachings of Sri Ramakrishna, Swami Vivekananda, and the ideals of the Ramakrishna Order. The ashrama also celebrates important festivals such as Durga Puja, Kali Puja, and Swami Vivekananda's birthday with great enthusiasm, attracting devotees from all over. Additionally, the ashrama organizes health camps, blood donation drives, and social service activities to serve the local community. The events at the Ramakrishna Mission Ashrama in Rajgir provide a platform for individuals to connect with their spiritual selves while contributing positively to society.
